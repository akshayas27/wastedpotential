ABOUT LOCKED POTENTIAL

An online Escape Room that is based on the topic of “Lockdown”. While metaphorically representing being locked inside a room, it also is a image of our reality i.e being trapped in our room due to the current situation worldwide; COVID-19.

You and your friends thought it would be a great idea to explore the different, lesser known, areas in Singapore. One day, taking the opportunity provided by a free weekend, you and your friends snuck out, roaming the winding streets and crossing many a crossroad, straying off, away from society. It was late at night, and the darkness seemed to be sinking in. The air hung low and the silence lay eerily thick, draping over you all as you trudged through. Not long after, you chanced upon a tall building, grey, looming over the street you were standing on. Eager at your find, and bound by your curiosity, you pushed open the old door. With every footstep, the floor creaked, every footfall echoed in the depths of its twisted hallways, in every window pane danced eerie shadows, whether yours or not was unknown to you. With a loud bang, the door swung shut behind you, locking you inside, the air grew thicker, plunging you all into a suffocating darkness. Now you’ve got to escape.
